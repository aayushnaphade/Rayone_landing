Rayone Favicon Generator

Instructions:

To generate favicon files from your logo:

  1. Visit RealFaviconGenerator.net
  2. Upload your logo file (assets/rayone-logo.png)
  3. Configure settings (recommended: use your brand color #9f09f7 for theme color)
  4. Download the package
  5. Extract the files to your assets/favicon directory
